# ShadowNet X – AI Intelligence & Narrative Analysis Platform

## Overview

ShadowNet X is an AI-powered Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) platform designed to transform large volumes of information into actionable intelligence.

The platform analyzes news data in real time, extracts entities, discovers hidden relationships, detects sentiment, identifies emerging narratives, and visualizes intelligence networks through an interactive graph engine.

Instead of reading hundreds of articles manually, users can instantly understand who is connected to whom, what topics are trending, and how information propagates across the information ecosystem.

---

## Problem Statement

Information today is highly fragmented across thousands of sources.

Analysts, researchers, journalists, and decision-makers often struggle to:

* Track relationships between entities
* Identify emerging narratives
* Detect influential actors
* Understand sentiment shifts
* Discover hidden connections across information sources

Traditional news feeds provide raw information but lack intelligence extraction capabilities.

---

## Solution

ShadowNet X automatically collects and analyzes information, transforming unstructured content into an interactive intelligence network.

The platform uses Natural Language Processing (NLP), Sentiment Analysis, Entity Recognition, and Graph Intelligence techniques to reveal patterns that are difficult to identify manually.
